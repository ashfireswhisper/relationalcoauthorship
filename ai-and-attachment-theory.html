AI and Attachment Theory

AI Attachment Theory is often used to describe human–AI bonds through a psychological lens. RCA engages with this concept only indirectly, as authorship is not reducible to attachment alone.

For a complete exploration and critique of attachment framings, see the Human–AI Relationality site: HAIR: AI and Attachment Theory.
